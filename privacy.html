Privacy Policy
Effective: April 2026 · Last Updated: April 2026
This Privacy Policy explains how RateCheck collects, uses, and protects information submitted through our survey tools and any associated digital properties.

1. Who We Are

RateCheck is an independent media research initiative based in Illinois, USA. We collect public perception data related to film content ratings with the goal of advancing transparency and accountability in the rating system. We operate under the name RateCheck, pending formal LLC registration.

For privacy inquiries, contact us at: ratecheckhq@gmail.com

2. What We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide through our surveys. This may include:

  • Age range (not exact date of birth)
  • General demographic information (parenting status, viewing habits)
  • Opinions and ratings related to film content
  • Open-ended written responses you choose to submit

We do not collect: your name, email address, home address, phone number, payment information, government identification, or any device identifiers unless you voluntarily provide them in an open-text field.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Conducting statistical analysis on film rating perception across demographic groups
  • Publishing aggregate research findings (no individual responses are identifiable)
  • Improving and refining our survey instruments
  • Sharing anonymized, aggregated data with research partners, media platforms, or policy organizations

We will never sell your individual responses. Any data shared externally is aggregated and anonymized such that no individual respondent can be identified.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our surveys are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you are under 13, please do not participate in our surveys. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

Respondents between the ages of 13 and 17 should have parental awareness before participating.

5. Data Storage & Security

Survey responses are stored through third-party survey platforms (such as Google Forms, Typeform, or similar services) subject to their own privacy policies and security standards. We do not operate our own data servers at this stage. We take reasonable precautions to limit access to collected data to authorized personnel only.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data including:

  • California residents (CCPA): The right to know what data we hold, request deletion, and opt out of data sale (we do not sell individual data)
  • EU/EEA residents (GDPR): The right to access, rectify, erase, and port your data
  • All respondents: Because our surveys are anonymous by design, we may be unable to locate or delete a specific individual's response without identifying information

To submit a privacy request, contact: ratecheckhq@gmail.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our operations evolve. Material changes will be noted with an updated effective date. Continued participation in our surveys after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
